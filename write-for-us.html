Topics we accept

We're a US-focused travel publication. Pitches should fit one or more of these categories:

Road Trips National Parks City Guides Adventure Travel Coastal Escapes Luxury Travel Budget Travel Family Travel Solo Travel Food & Local Culture Travel Gear & Packing Sustainable Travel

Editorial guidelines

  • 1
    Original & unpublished. Articles must be written for TravelVista USA and not appear elsewhere, including on your own blog.
  • 2
    1,200–2,000 words. Long enough to be genuinely useful, structured with clear subheadings.
  • 3
    First-hand experience. We prioritize pitches from writers who have actually visited the destination.
  • 4
    High-quality images. Include 3–5 original or properly licensed photos with credit.
  • 5
    Practical detail. Costs, timing, logistics and honest recommendations, not generic filler.
  • 6
    Up to two links. One author-bio link and one contextual link; no link farms or unrelated promotion.

What we don't accept

  • AI-generated or heavily spun content submitted as original writing
  • Listicles with no first-hand detail or personal insight
  • Paid link placements disguised as editorial content
  • Content unrelated to US or North American travel
  • Duplicate or previously published articles
