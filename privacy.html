Privacy Policy

1. Information we collect

When you use our contact form, submit a guest post pitch, or subscribe to our newsletter, we collect the information you provide directly, such as your name, email address and message content.

2. How we use your information

We use this information to respond to inquiries, review guest post pitches, and send newsletter updates to subscribers who opt in. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Form submissions

Our contact and guest-post forms are processed through Formspree, a third-party form service. Submitted data is handled according to Formspree's own privacy practices in addition to ours.

4. Cookies

This site may use basic cookies or local browser storage to remember preferences such as dark mode. We don't use tracking cookies for advertising purposes.

5. Data retention

We retain contact form and pitch submissions only as long as needed to respond to your inquiry or evaluate your pitch.

6. Your rights

You may request that we delete any personal information you've submitted to us by emailing hello@travelvistausa.com.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. Material changes will be reflected with an updated revision date.
